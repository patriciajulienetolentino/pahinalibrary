Pahina Library - Online Library Book List

For the Pahina Library Online Book List, I was tasked with creating a visually appealing and user-friendly interface to display a comprehensive collection of books available in the library. The goal was to provide an accessible, digital version of the libraryâ€™s catalog, making it easier for users to browse, search, and access information about each book.

Key features included:

  • Book Categorization: Organized books by genres, authors, and themes, allowing users to filter and find books of interest quickly.
  • Search Functionality: Developed an easy-to-use search feature, enabling users to find specific titles or authors effortlessly.
  • Book Details: For each book, included vital information such as the title, author, summary, and availability status.
  • Responsive Design: Ensured the online book list was mobile-friendly, providing a seamless browsing experience across devices.
